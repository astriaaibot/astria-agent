Common Questions

How long does setup take?
Most customers are up and running within 1-2 hours. Our onboarding wizard walks you through ICP definition, email setup, and calendar integration.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! All new customers get a 7-day free trial with no credit card required. Full access to all platform features.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) through Stripe. We also offer invoice billing for enterprise customers.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes. You can change your plan anytime from your dashboard. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle. If you upgrade, we'll prorate the new amount.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your lead data and campaign history will be deleted within 30 days of cancellation. You can export all your data before canceling.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS), secure databases, and comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other privacy regulations. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
