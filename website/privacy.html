Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Astria ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the astria.fun website and Astria service. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service and the choices you have associated with that data.

2. Information Collection and Use

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account with Astria, we collect:

  • Email address
  • Business name
  • Company information (industry, size, location)
  • Billing address
  • Payment information (processed securely through Stripe)

2.2 Lead Data

Our service collects and processes prospect/lead data from public sources including Google Maps, Yelp, local directories, and your own CRM. This data includes:

  • Business names and contact information
  • Phone numbers and email addresses
  • Business addresses and industry categories
  • Website URLs and social media profiles

2.3 Email and Communication Data

When you use our email sending and reply management features, we process:

  • Outbound emails sent on your behalf
  • Inbound replies from prospects
  • Email engagement metrics (opens, clicks, replies)
  • Calendar availability and booking data

2.4 Usage and Analytics

We automatically collect information about your interaction with our service, including:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Campaign performance and conversion metrics
  • System activity logs

3. Use of Data

Astria uses the collected data for various purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the Astria service
  • To find, score, and qualify sales leads
  • To generate personalized email outreach on your behalf
  • To track email engagement and prospect responses
  • To provide analytics and reporting
  • To send service notifications and updates
  • To improve our algorithms and services
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Lead Data Handling

Astria handles prospect data with care and in compliance with applicable laws:

  • We only use prospect data to generate leads on your behalf
  • We comply with CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CCPA, and other email regulations
  • Leads are used exclusively by your account and not shared with other customers
  • Data is securely stored in encrypted databases
  • We use opt-out and consent mechanisms where required by law

5. Email Domain and Warmup

When you connect your email domain to Astria:

  • We configure DNS records (SPF, DKIM, DMARC) for email authentication
  • We may use email warming services to improve deliverability
  • You retain full control of your email domain and data
  • We do not access or read personal emails in your mailbox

6. Third-Party Service Providers

Astria uses third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Anthropic Claude: AI/ML for lead scoring and email generation
  • Instantly.ai: Email delivery and warmup
  • Cal.com: Calendar and booking functionality

These providers are contractually obligated to use your data only as necessary to provide their services and maintain its confidentiality and security.

7. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

We implement industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) for data in transit and at rest. Access to customer data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

8. Data Retention

Astria retains your account data as long as your subscription is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Your lead data and campaign history will be deleted within 30 days
  • Billing records will be retained as required by law (7 years)
  • You may request data export before deletion

9. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request all personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at astriaaibot@gmail.com with your request.

10. Compliance

Astria complies with applicable data protection regulations including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • CAN-SPAM Act
  • CASL (Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation)

11. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your login information
  • Understand how you use our service
  • Improve our website performance

You can control cookie preferences in your browser settings.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the service constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
