### Lead Data Handling
- Prospects are found from public sources only
- Used exclusively for customer's outreach
- Compliance with email regulations (CAN-SPAM, GDPR)
- No sharing with other customers

### Third-Party Services

1. **Supabase** - Database and authentication
2. **Stripe** - Payment processing
