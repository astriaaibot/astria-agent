Questions?

How quickly do I see results?

First leads appear in your inbox by Day 3. Appointments typically book within 1-2 weeks as email warmup completes.

What if I don't get appointments?

We adjust strategy: new search terms, different email angles, expanded geography. 30-day money-back guarantee.

Can you target my area?

Yes. Any city, county, or zip code. We define your ideal customer profile during onboarding.

Do you email my existing leads?

No. Astria only emails prospects NOT already in your system. We verify before sending.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Month-to-month, no contracts. We'd love to keep you, but no hard feelings if it's not right.

How does appointment booking work?

Interested prospects click a link, see your calendar, and book directly. Appointments appear on your calendar instantly.
